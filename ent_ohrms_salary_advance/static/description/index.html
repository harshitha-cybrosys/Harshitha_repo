Frequently Asked Questions

Can the module handle multiple salary advance requests?

Yes, the system can manage and track multiple salary advance requests for different employees simultaneously. However, each employee is limited to one approved advance per month.

How is the advance salary deducted from future payrolls?

The system automatically deducts the advance salary from the employee's payslip at the time of payslip computation based on the configured salary rules in the salary structure.

Who can approve a salary advance request?

The process involves two levels of approval. First, an HR Manager reviews and approves the request. Then, the Accounts Department provides the final approval and processes the payment journal entry.

Is there a limit on the advance salary amount?

Yes, the advance amount is validated against the employee's contract wage. By default it cannot exceed the monthly wage, but HR Managers can override this by enabling the "Exceed than Maximum" option on the request.
