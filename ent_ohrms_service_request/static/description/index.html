Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the Enterprise HRMS Service Request module?

This module streamlines the management of employee service requests, enabling HR teams to track, process, and resolve requests efficiently while maintaining transparency and communication throughout the workflow.

How does the service request approval process work?

Once an employee submits a service request, the administrator or HR Manager assigns it to an executor. The executor performs the service and marks it as 'Checked'. The requesting employee can then approve or reject the completed service.

Who can assign a service executor?

Only users with the Administrator or HR Manager role can assign a service executor to a submitted request. The executor must have the 'Service Executer' role configured under their user settings.
