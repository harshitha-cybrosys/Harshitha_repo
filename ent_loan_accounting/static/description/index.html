Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the Enterprise OpenHRMS Loan Accounting module?

This module manages employee loan requests with a double-layer approval from the HR and Accounting departments, while also generating accounting journal entries automatically.

How does the loan approval process work?

Loan requests go through HR approval first. If the "Loan Approval From Accounting Department" option is enabled in settings, a second approval from the Accounting department is required before the loan is processed and journal entries are created.

How are loan installments deducted from payslips?

When generating a payslip, all pending loan installments for that month are automatically listed under Salary Inputs. Once the payslip is confirmed, the installments are marked as PAID and a corresponding journal entry is posted.

What accounting fields need to be configured before approving a loan?

Before approving a loan, you must set the Loan Account, Treasury Account, and Journal on the loan request form. These are used to generate the debit and credit lines in the account move.
