Key Highlights

HR Dashboard

An overview of key HR metrics, such as employee details, attendance, leave status, and payroll insights. It helps HR teams efficiently manage and monitor workforce activities in real-time.

Employee Reminders

Ensures timely notifications for critical tasks like document renewals, contract expirations, and upcoming evaluations. It helps maintain compliance and streamlines HR workflows efficiently.

Salary Advance

Allows employees to request salary advances with ease, while automating approval workflows and deductions. It ensures seamless management of advances, improving financial flexibility for employees.

HR Multi Company

Effortlessly manage employees across multiple branches with streamlined workflows, centralized data access, and branch-specific configurations, ensuring smooth operations and consistent HR processes across all locations.

Loan Management

Easily configure various loan policies tailored to your organization's needs, assign approval authorities for streamlined decision-making, and efficiently manage the sanctioning and disbursement of employee loans.

HR Announcements

Efficiently manage all official communications, including announcements, greetings, warnings, and rewards, to foster transparency and employee engagement across the organization.

HR Documents Management

Streamline the management of employee documents directly within the Employee Form view, enabling easy upload, storage, tracking, and retrieval of essential files all in one centralized location.

Automation on Leave Request Mails

Automatically generate leave requests from incoming emails, simplifying the leave application process by capturing relevant details directly from the email content with minimal manual effort.

Resignation

Empower employees to draft and submit their resignation statements directly through the system. Higher authorities can review, accept, or reject the resignation, ensuring transparent documentation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the Enterprise HRMS Core app by Cybrosys?

The Enterprise HRMS Core app is designed to streamline and enhance HR operations within an organization. It offers advanced features such as employee management, payroll integration, document tracking, attendance monitoring, leave management, and loan handling, providing a comprehensive solution for all HR needs.

Can the module handle multi-branch employee management?

Yes, the module allows organizations to manage employees across multiple branches efficiently. It supports branch-specific configurations, centralized data management, and seamless integration to ensure consistent HR processes across all locations.

Does the module support automated workflows for HR tasks?

Absolutely. The Enterprise HRMS Core app automates various HR workflows, such as leave request generation from emails, loan policy approvals, and document renewal reminders, reducing manual effort and ensuring accuracy in day-to-day HR activities.
