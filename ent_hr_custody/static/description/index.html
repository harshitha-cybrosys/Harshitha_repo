Custody Request Management

Property Tracking

Efficiently manage and track all company assets — including product-linked properties with stock integration — while they are in an employee's custody.

Renewal & Return Workflow

Employees can request custody renewals to extend the return date. Managers approve or refuse with reasons, maintaining a full audit trail.

The system automatically sends email reminders to employees when the return date approaches, with a direct link to renew the custody from the mail.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should you choose the HR Custody Management module by Cybrosys?

The HR Custody Management module by Cybrosys simplifies tracking and managing employee asset custody in Odoo. It streamlines assigning, returning, and monitoring company-owned assets with ease and efficiency, while maintaining a full audit trail through the chatter.

How does the custody renewal process work?

When a custody is in the Approved state, the employee can click the Renew button to request an extended return date. The request moves back to Waiting For Approval state. The HR manager can approve the new date or refuse it with a reason. If refused, the original return date remains in effect.

Does the module support product-based custody with stock tracking?

Yes. Custody properties can be linked to a specific product and stock location. When a custody request is approved, the available quantity in stock is automatically reduced. When the item is returned, the quantity is restored. This keeps inventory accurate without any manual updates.

How does the return date reminder work?

A scheduled cron job runs daily and checks all approved custody records. When the return date is due or approaching, the system automatically sends an email reminder to the employee's work email with a direct link to the custody record where they can initiate a renewal if needed.

Can I see all custody records of an employee from the employee form?

Yes. Two smart buttons are added to the employee form — one showing the total count of all custody contracts and another showing the count of currently approved equipment in custody. Clicking either button takes you directly to the filtered list of records for that employee.
