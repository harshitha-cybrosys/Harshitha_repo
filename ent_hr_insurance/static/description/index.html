Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the Enterprise Open HRMS Employee Insurance Module?

The Enterprise Open HRMS Employee Insurance Module allows organizations to manage and track insurance policies related to their employees, ensuring seamless integration with HR and payroll operations in Odoo.

How does insurance deduction reflect on the payslip?

The module adds dedicated salary rules that automatically compute insurance deductions based on the employee's active policies. These deductions appear as separate lines on the payslip, giving full visibility of insurance-related salary adjustments.

What policy coverage types are supported?

The module supports both Monthly and Yearly policy coverage. The policy end date is automatically computed based on the selected coverage type, and the insurance state updates to Active or Expired accordingly.

Where can I view all insurance policies linked to an employee?

Active insurance policies are displayed directly on the Employee form view under the Insurance tab. You can also navigate to Employees → Insurance for a full list view of all insurance records across all employees.
